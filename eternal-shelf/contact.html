Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from the Settings screen in the app, or contact us via email and we will process your request within 48 hours.
Is Eternal Shelf really free?
Yes! Eternal Shelf is completely free with no ads, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. All books are from Project Gutenberg's public domain collection.
Can I read books offline?
Yes, books you've opened are cached on your device for offline reading. You can also explicitly download books for offline access through the book detail page.
How do I report a bug or issue?
Please email us with a description of the issue, your device model, and operating system version. Screenshots are helpful if applicable.
Can I request a specific book?
Our library is based on Project Gutenberg's collection. If a book is in the public domain and available on Project Gutenberg, it should be available in Eternal Shelf. If you notice a book is missing, please let us know.
How do I request my personal data?
Under GDPR and similar privacy laws, you have the right to request a copy of your personal data. Please email us with your request and we will respond within 30 days.

Before Contacting Us

To help us assist you faster, please include the following information in your email:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15, Samsung Galaxy S24)
  • Operating system version (e.g., iOS 18.1, Android 15)
  • App version (found in Settings)
  • Description of your issue or question
  • Screenshots if relevant
